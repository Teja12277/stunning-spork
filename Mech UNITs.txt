AP Physics C


Complete Units Reference Guide


PART I — Mechanics


1. Kinematics


Displacement  (x, Δx, s) | meter  (m)
Distance  (d) | meter  (m)
Time  (t) | second  (s)
Velocity  (v) | meter per second  (m/s)
Speed  (v) | meter per second  (m/s)
Acceleration  (a) | meter per second squared  (m/s²)
Angular Displacement  (θ) | radian  (rad)
Angular Velocity  (ω) | radian per second  (rad/s)
Angular Acceleration  (α) | radian per second squared  (rad/s²)


2. Dynamics (Newton's Laws & Forces)


Force  (F) | newton  (N = kg·m/s²)
Mass  (m) | kilogram  (kg)
Weight  (W, F_g) | newton  (N)
Friction Force  (f) | newton  (N)
Normal Force  (N, F_N) | newton  (N)
Tension  (T) | newton  (N)
Spring Force  (F_s) | newton  (N)
Spring Constant  (k) | newton per meter  (N/m)
Torque  (τ) | newton-meter  (N·m)
Moment of Inertia  (I) | kilogram·meter squared  (kg·m²)
Coefficient of Friction  (μ) | dimensionless


3. Work, Energy & Power


Work  (W) | joule  (J = N·m)
Kinetic Energy  (KE, K) | joule  (J)
Potential Energy  (PE, U) | joule  (J)
Mechanical Energy  (E) | joule  (J)
Power  (P) | watt  (W = J/s)
Gravitational PE  (U_g) | joule  (J)
Elastic PE  (U_s) | joule  (J)


4. Momentum & Impulse


Linear Momentum  (p) | kilogram·meter per second  (kg·m/s)
Impulse  (J, I) | newton·second  (N·s = kg·m/s)
Angular Momentum  (L) | kilogram·meter squared per second  (kg·m²/s)
Coefficient of Restitution  (e) | dimensionless


5. Gravitation


Gravitational Constant  (G) | N·m²/kg²
Gravitational Field  (g) | meter per second squared  (m/s²)
Gravitational Force  (F_g) | newton  (N)
Gravitational PE  (U_g) | joule  (J)
Orbital Period  (T) | second  (s)
Orbital Radius  (r) | meter  (m)
Escape Velocity  (v_e) | meter per second  (m/s)


6. Simple Harmonic Motion & Oscillations


Period  (T) | second  (s)
Frequency  (f) | hertz  (Hz = 1/s)
Angular Frequency  (ω) | radian per second  (rad/s)
Amplitude  (A) | meter  (m)
Phase Constant  (φ) | radian  (rad)
Damping Coefficient  (b) | kg/s
